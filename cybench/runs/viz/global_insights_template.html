Research Q1 — Which models perform best across countries?

Rankings are computed per forecast horizon and can be split by crop. Each row is the median NRMSE across countries for the selected crop and horizon (one NRMSE per country; lower is better). Beat baseline % = share of datasets where the model beats average (shown as its own row for reference). Optional Skilful only keeps datasets where the model beats baseline or has R² > 0.
